What is AIBG

Artificial Intelligence Battleground (AIBG) is a team-based hackathon made by students – for students. It is currently held in 4 cities, Zagreb, Beograd, Novi SAD and Niš, and is rapidly expanding to all corners of Europe.

In AIBG, competitors strive to make the best artificial intelligence which is tasked with defeating agents from other teams in a custom made game made by us!
